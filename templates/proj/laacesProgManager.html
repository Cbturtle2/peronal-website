Here is a mission goal of this project:
For this second LaACES project of NSULA/LSMSA collaboration, Team DemonSats plans to launch a payload by atmospheric sounding balloon to 100,000 ft (30,480 m) to explore saturated vapor pressure as a function of altitude and by constructing a MegaSat payload to measure atmospheric temperature, pressure, % relative humidity, and altitude.
